# E-E-A-T Evaluation Rubric

Structured evaluation of Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness and Trustworthiness.

## 1. Experience — 0-25 points

| Score | Level | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| 20-25 | Excellent | Direct personal experience, demonstrated and verifiable. Concrete examples, case studies based on real experience. |
| 15-19 | Good | Clear professional experience. Some practical examples or references to previous projects. |
| 10-14 | Acceptable | Domain knowledge with some practical references. Lacks depth. |
| 5-9 | Weak | General knowledge, little visible practical experience. Content mainly theoretical. |
| 0-4 | Very weak | No demonstrable experience. Secondary or copied content. |

### Verification points
- Author names their specific accomplishments
- Inclusion of concrete details from previous projects
- Case studies or examples from real experience
- Clear description of role and responsibilities

---

## 2. Expertise — 0-25 points

| Score | Level | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| 20-25 | Excellent | Demonstrated pointed expertise. Correct technical terminology. Deep and nuanced understanding. |
| 15-19 | Good | Good domain understanding. Correct use of terminology. |
| 10-14 | Acceptable | Solid general knowledge. Terminology generally correct. |
| 5-9 | Weak | Basic knowledge. Minor terminology errors. |
| 0-4 | Very weak | Insufficient knowledge. Significant factual errors. |

### Verification points
- No factual or terminological errors
- Correct and precise technical explanations
- Ability to explore subject nuances
- References to advanced or specialized concepts

---

## 3. Authoritativeness (Authority) — 0-25 points

| Score | Level | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| 20-25 | Excellent | Author recognized as expert. Citations by other sources. Publications in authoritative publications. |
| 15-19 | Good | Established credibility. Some publications or domain recognition. |
| 10-14 | Acceptable | Recognized by peers. Some publications or mentions. |
| 5-9 | Weak | Basic presence in domain. Little external recognition. |
| 0-4 | Very weak | No established authority. |

### Verification points
- Complete author bio with accreditations
- Links to portfolio or previous publications
- Clear professional affiliations
- Relevant certifications or degrees
- sameAs property linking to external profiles (LinkedIn, etc.)

---

## 4. Trustworthiness (Reliability) — 0-25 points

| Score | Level | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| 20-25 | Excellent | Transparent and verifiable content. Sources cited explicitly with links. Disclosure of conflicts of interest. |
| 15-19 | Good | Generally reliable content. Most sources cited. |
| 10-14 | Acceptable | Sources mentioned but not always with direct links. |
| 5-9 | Weak | Vague or incomplete sources. Low transparency. |
| 0-4 | Very weak | No sources cited. Doubtful author identity. |

### Verification points
- Sources cited with direct and accessible links
- Author clearly identified with contact
- Explicit disclosures of affiliations
- Publication/update date visible

---

## Total E-E-A-T Score

**Formula:** (Experience + Expertise + Authority + Trustworthiness) / 4

| Score | Level | Interpretation |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| 22-25 | Excellent | Highly trustworthy content, suitable for YMYL subjects |
| 18-21 | Good | Reliable content for most subjects |
| 14-17 | Acceptable | Published content but with caveats |
| 10-13 | Weak | Major improvements recommended |
| 0-9 | Very weak | Major revision necessary |

---

## YMYL Subjects (Your Money Your Life)

Minimum requirement: E-E-A-T Score >= 20

- **Health**: medical advice, nutrition, supplements, mental health
- **Finance**: investment, retirement, credit, taxes, insurance
- **Security/Legal**: legal advice, home security
- **Major decisions**: real estate, education, career

---

## Standard goals

- Blog posts: E-E-A-T Score >= 18
- Authority articles: E-E-A-T Score >= 22
- YMYL content: E-E-A-T Score >= 20
- Technical guides: E-E-A-T Score >= 19
